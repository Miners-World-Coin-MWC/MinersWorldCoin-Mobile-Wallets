# How to Sideload Miners World Coin Wallet on iPhone (iOS)

This guide explains how to install the Miners World Coin Wallet on iPhone using sideloading.

Because the app is not currently distributed through the Apple App Store, it must be manually installed using your own Apple ID.

---

# Important Information

- You will need:
  - A Windows PC or Mac
  - An iPhone or iPad
  - A Lightning or USB-C cable
  - Your Apple ID

- Free Apple IDs work fine, but:
  - the app will expire after 7 days
  - you must refresh/reinstall it weekly

- Never download IPA files from unofficial sources.

---

# Recommended Method: AltStore

AltStore is currently the easiest and safest way to sideload apps on iOS.

---

# Step 1 — Download AltStore

Visit the official website:

https://altstore.io/

Download AltServer for:
- Windows
- macOS

Install it on your computer.

---

# Step 2 — Prepare Your iPhone

On your iPhone:

1. Open Settings
2. Go to:
   - Privacy & Security
3. Enable:
   - Developer Mode (if prompted later)

Connect your iPhone to your computer using USB.

Trust the computer if prompted.

---

# Step 3 — Install AltStore to Your iPhone

## Windows

Requirements:
- iTunes installed from Apple website
- iCloud installed from Apple website

Do NOT use Microsoft Store versions.

Launch AltServer.

From the system tray:

AltServer > Install AltStore

Select your iPhone.

Enter your Apple ID credentials when prompted.

---

## macOS

Launch AltServer.

From the menu bar:

AltServer > Install AltStore

Select your device.

Enter your Apple ID credentials.

---

# Step 4 — Trust the Developer Profile

On your iPhone:

Go to:

Settings > General > VPN & Device Management

Find your Apple ID profile.

Tap:

Trust

# Step 6 — Install the Wallet Using AltStore

On your iPhone:

1. Open AltStore
2. Go to:
   My Apps
3. Tap:
   +
4. Select the downloaded IPA file
5. Wait for installation to complete

The wallet should now appear on your home screen.

---

# Keeping the App Working

Free Apple IDs expire every 7 days.

To refresh:

1. Connect iPhone to computer
2. Open AltStore
3. Refresh the app before expiration

---

# Troubleshooting

## "Untrusted Developer"

Go to:

Settings > General > VPN & Device Management

Trust your Apple ID profile.

---

## App Crashes Immediately

Try:
- reinstalling the IPA
- refreshing AltStore
- rebooting the device

---

## AltStore Won't Install

Make sure:
- iPhone is trusted
- cable works properly
- iTunes/iCloud are installed correctly
- Wi-Fi sync is enabled in iTunes

---
